About Us

Our History

Brite Visuals started off as a non-profit-for-fun crew of friends that loved to tell stories and inspire people through media production, whilst donating all our proceeds to the American Cancer Society. Although we continue to be involved in charity, we since then have expanded into a business looking to create new films and videos focused on innovating the way stories are told. We know how to make videos, and we are always looking to try something new.

Our Values

To create a lasting BOND within our crew and with any outside organization we work with. To REACH out to our audience emotionally along with promote those that deserve the exposure. To INSPIRE people in their pursuit of their goals with our narratives.To TELL stories as effectively and efficiently as possible with all the tools we have: cinematography, writing, sound are only a few of the proficiencies we have at our disposal. And most importantly, to ENTERTAIN with the quality, aesthetic, passion, and empathy.

Our Vision

No matter how good our work is, we will always strive to improve. No matter how far we go, we keep going. Our goal is to always have our next work be out best work, and keep our standards high for every production.
